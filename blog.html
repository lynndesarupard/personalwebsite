BIRTHDAY GIRL

Aspen's 7th Birthday, June 19, 2023

What a day..

Today was my little girl's birthday. She turned seven years old. How is it that life goes by so fast, right before our eyes? I started her day with birthday pancakes. My girl loves pancakes! It's her favorite food for any meal.


For lunch, she requested Mexican food. We took her to Habanero where they sang to her, and brought her a huge dessert that she shared with all of us, including her brother. For those of you that have kids with siblings, you know how major this is.


Later in the evening we still did all our normal Monday routines by running to tumble practice, and swim practice after that. As I tucked Aspen in for bed, she told me that she had the best day ever. I'm so thankful that our kiddos are not hard to please. We will celebrate her BIRTHDAY with friends and family on Saturday.
